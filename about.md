## Class Description

This course is an introduction to the sport of Jugger. It will cover
the theory and practice of all aspects of the game, as well as
off-field topics such as history and equipment construction.

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, students should develop a strong
understanding of the rules, mechanics, and history of the sport of
Jugger.

Students will be equipped to study, analyze, and practice all aspects
of the game throughout lineups, midgames, and endgames, plus off-field
skills such as refereeing. An emphasis will also be maintained on
injury prevention and safety, as long-term training of running and
agility-heavy sports in general can be dangerous if not done
correctly.
